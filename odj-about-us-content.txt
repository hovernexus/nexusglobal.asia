## Company Description (English)

Foshan ODJ Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd. --Located in Gaoming District, Foshan City, the company specializes in R & D and manufacturing corrugated board feeding machine for 12 years, including automatic feeding machine and semi-automatic feeding machine respectively. In addition, it also produces palletizing robot system and AGV intelligent storage vehicle (Nansha Branch of Oudejia Intelligent Technology Co., Ltd., Yuwotou Town, Nansha District, Guangzhou City).

It can be used with watermark printing machine and flatbed die-cutting machine in carton factory, and can be customized according to the special needs of customers, providing optimized corrugated board conveying scheme for customers. In order to further improve the performance of all aspects of the feeder, Oudejia has invested a lot of money and manpower for a long time, focusing on the configuration of paper feeder components and the improvement of functionality, stability and safety in all aspects.
