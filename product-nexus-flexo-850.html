NEXUS-FLEXO 850

Medium Format Flexographic Printing Press

The NEXUS-FLEXO 850 series is the perfect solution to step into modern flexible packaging production. Combining advanced printing technology with user-friendly operation, this machine delivers exceptional print quality and productivity for medium-format applications.

  • Maximum output with printing speeds of up to 300 m/min
  • Top price/performance ratio and maximum productivity
  • High substrate flexibility for various packaging materials
  • Short makeready times for fast job changes
  • Advanced automation and easy handling

Main Features

High-Speed Performance

Achieve printing speeds up to 300 m/min with consistent quality. Advanced servo drive system ensures precise registration and minimal waste.

Superior Print Quality

Advanced anilox roller technology and precision doctor blade system deliver sharp, vibrant prints with excellent color consistency.

Flexible Substrate Handling

Process a wide range of materials including PE, PP, PET, paper, and laminated films with thicknesses from 12μm to 300μm.

Quick Job Changeover

Automated plate mounting and ink system cleaning reduce makeready time to less than 15 minutes, maximizing production efficiency.

Advanced Automation

PLC-controlled operation with touchscreen interface. Automatic tension control, register adjustment, and color management system.

Energy Efficient

LED UV curing system and optimized drying technology reduce energy consumption by up to 40% compared to traditional systems.

Applications

Food Packaging

Flexible pouches, snack bags, candy wrappers, and food labels with FDA-compliant inks.

Beverage Labels

Shrink sleeves, pressure-sensitive labels, and wrap-around labels for bottles and cans.

Pharmaceutical Packaging

Blister packs, sachets, and medical device packaging with high-resolution printing.

Personal Care Products

Cosmetic labels, shampoo pouches, and wet wipe packaging with vibrant graphics.

Industrial Labels

Warning labels, instruction labels, and product identification tags.

Retail Packaging

Shopping bags, gift wrap, and promotional packaging materials.
