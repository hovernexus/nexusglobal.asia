NEXUS-FLEXO 850

Medium Format Flexographic Printing Press

The NEXUS-FLEXO 850 series is the perfect solution to step into modern flexible packaging production.

  • Maximum output with printing speeds of up to 300 m/min
  • Top price/performance ratio and maximum productivity
  • High substrate flexibility for various packaging materials
  • Short makeready times for fast job changes
  • Advanced automation and easy handling
Main Features Applications Technical Data Downloads Contact

Efficient and Successful in Modern Flexible Packaging Production

The flexographic presses of the NEXUS-FLEXO 850 series offer the perfect platform for a successful start into modern flexible packaging production. They meet all the requirements and quality parameters for modern and cost-oriented production of flexible packaging products.

They handle web widths of up to 850 mm and stand out thanks to their ease of operation and short make-ready times for fast job changes. The advanced automation features ensure consistent quality and maximum productivity.

With integrated quality control systems and intelligent tension management, the NEXUS-FLEXO 850 provides even more efficiency and reliability for your production needs.

Core Functional Modules

The advanced unwinding system features automatic tension control with real-time monitoring and adjustment. This ensures consistent web tension throughout the printing process, preventing defects and maintaining print quality.

Each printing unit is equipped with servo-driven anilox rollers and automatic register control. The modular design allows for easy maintenance and quick color changes, maximizing uptime and productivity.

The integrated AI quality inspection system continuously monitors print quality, detecting defects in real-time. Automatic alerts and adjustments minimize waste and ensure consistent output quality throughout the production run.

The rewinding station features automatic core loading and tension control for perfect roll formation. Optional inline slitting capabilities allow for immediate roll division, reducing post-processing time and improving workflow efficiency.
