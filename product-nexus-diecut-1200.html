NEXUS-DIECUT 1200

High-Speed Rotary Die-Cutting Machine

The NEXUS-DIECUT 1200 represents the pinnacle of die-cutting technology for corrugated packaging production. Engineered for maximum precision and productivity, this machine delivers exceptional cutting quality at industry-leading speeds, making it the ideal choice for high-volume production environments.

  • Ultra-high speed operation up to 12,000 sheets per hour
  • Precision cutting with ±0.5mm accuracy
  • Advanced servo-driven feeding system
  • Automatic waste stripping and collection
  • Quick die change system reduces downtime

Main Features

High-Speed Performance

Process up to 12,000 sheets per hour with consistent quality. Advanced servo motors ensure precise timing and registration throughout the production run.

Precision Cutting

Achieve cutting accuracy of ±0.5mm with our patented die-cutting cylinder system. Perfect for complex shapes and tight tolerances.

Automatic Waste Removal

Integrated waste stripping and collection system automatically removes scrap material, reducing manual labor and improving efficiency.

Quick Die Change

Magnetic die mounting system allows die changes in under 10 minutes. Pre-register system ensures immediate production start.

Versatile Material Handling

Process corrugated board from E-flute to triple-wall, as well as solid board and plastic sheets up to 10mm thickness.

Smart Control System

Touchscreen HMI with production monitoring, job memory storage, and remote diagnostics capability for maximum uptime.

Applications

Corrugated Boxes

Regular slotted containers (RSC), die-cut boxes, and specialty packaging with complex shapes.

Display Packaging

Point-of-purchase displays, counter displays, and retail-ready packaging with intricate designs.

E-commerce Packaging

Mailer boxes, shipping containers, and protective packaging for online retail.

Food Service Packaging

Pizza boxes, takeout containers, and food trays with custom shapes and ventilation holes.

Industrial Packaging

Heavy-duty boxes, automotive parts packaging, and electronics packaging.

Specialty Applications

Gift boxes, presentation packaging, and promotional materials with unique die-cut features.
