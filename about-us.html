About NEXUS Global

Building the Future of Corrugated Packaging Through Innovation, Integration, and Global Collaboration

Our Story

Pioneering the Digital Transformation of Corrugated Packaging Industry

Founded in Singapore, NEXUS Global Holdings Inc. represents a new paradigm in the corrugated packaging equipment industry. We are not just a marketplace—we are an intelligent ecosystem that connects manufacturers, suppliers, and end-users across the globe.

Our vision is to revolutionize the corrugated packaging industry by creating the world's first comprehensive digital platform that integrates equipment sourcing, technical consultation, financing solutions, and after-sales support. We believe that the future of manufacturing lies in collaboration, transparency, and intelligent decision-making.

With deep roots in Asia and a global perspective, NEXUS Global serves as a bridge between cutting-edge technology providers and forward-thinking manufacturers. We leverage AI-powered matching algorithms, comprehensive equipment databases, and industry expertise to help our clients make informed decisions that drive profitability and sustainability.

Our platform features over 500+ certified equipment suppliers, 10,000+ product listings, and serves clients in more than 80 countries. We are committed to making high-quality corrugated packaging equipment accessible, affordable, and optimized for every production need.
